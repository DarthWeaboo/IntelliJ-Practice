About Us

Education Matters was started in 2005 by Nick Bratt. Nick Bratt has an undying dedication to improving education for children around the world. Through our hard work we equip teachers and parents around the world with the tools they need to give their children the tools necessary to get the most out of their education. We believe that by doing this, we are creating a better, and brighter future for us all.
